## Gaps Found (2 items)

### GAP-01: DATA-02 — liveWorkoutStrain has no display consumer

**Severity:** medium  
**Success criterion:** "The strain tile on the workout screen updates at most every few seconds during an active session, driven by GooseStrainAccumulator receiving HR samples"  
**Actual state:** `GooseStrainAccumulator` computes realtime Edwards Zone Load and publishes to `GooseAppModel.liveWorkoutStrain`. Zero SwiftUI views read this property. The "strain tile on the workout screen" referenced in the success criterion does not exist.  
**Minimum fix:** Add a `Text(String(format: "Strain: %.1f", model.liveWorkoutStrain))` label or equivalent tile to `FitnessLiveWorkoutViews.swift` during active workout sessions.

### GAP-02: DATA-03 — ManualWorkoutEntrySheet has no entry point

**Severity:** medium  
**Success criterion:** "A Manual Workout Entry sheet allows the user to log a workout with sport tag, duration, and perceived effort"  
**Actual state:** `ManualWorkoutEntrySheet` is fully built and `workout.upsert` is wired correctly. However, no view presents the sheet (no `.sheet(isPresented:) { ManualWorkoutEntrySheet() }` call exists anywhere).  
**Minimum fix:** Add a "Log Workout" button/row in `HealthView`'s Explore Health section (or Fitness tab) that presents `ManualWorkoutEntrySheet` as a `.sheet`.
