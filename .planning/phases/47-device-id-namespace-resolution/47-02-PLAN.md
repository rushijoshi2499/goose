Wire the CoreBluetooth peripheral UUID through the iOS pipeline: expose it on the BLE client at connect, persist the UUID↔device_model mapping in UserDefaults, propagate it to the capture write queue (race-safe, captured at enqueue), and confirm the upload payload carries it.

Purpose: iOS half of DEVID-02 — the UUID extracted from CoreBluetooth becomes the consistent identifier that lands in `raw_evidence.device_uuid` (via the queue → Rust bridge) and in the upload payload (via the bridge response from Plan 01). Implements D-04, D-05, D-01, D-02, D-03.
Output: device_uuid captured on connect, persisted, written to SQLite per-frame, and uploaded.
</objective>
