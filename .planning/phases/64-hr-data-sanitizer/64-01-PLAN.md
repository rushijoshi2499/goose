<objective>
Add a Swift-side HR spike filter (`GooseHRSanitizer`) between raw BLE heart-rate values and both consumers (the live HR display and `HeartRateSeriesStore`), matching WHOOP's `WHPHeartRateDataSanitizer`. Physiologically impossible samples (outside 25–220 BPM) are rejected at a single chokepoint, logged via OSLog, and counted in a debug counter surfaced in More > Debug.

Purpose: Prevents spike artefacts from corrupting the live HR UI, the resting-HR estimate, the HRV window, and downstream Rust algorithms — a known WHOOP parity gap from the Ghidra cross-compare (HR-SAN-01).
Output: New `GooseHRSanitizer.swift`; `recordLiveHeartRate` gated through it; `hrSpikeCount` observable on `GooseAppModel`; spike-count row in More > Debug; build clean.
</objective>
