<objective>
Transfer ownership of HealthDataStore from AppShellView into GooseAppModel. The store becomes a strong `let` constant initialised once during model init, injected into the SwiftUI environment from GooseSwiftApp. AppShellView stops creating and managing the store lifecycle.

Purpose: Eliminates the circular weak-reference pattern (AppShellView owns store, AppShellView sets model.healthStore as weak, model calls back through optional). After this plan the ownership graph is acyclic: GooseSwiftApp -> GooseAppModel --owns--> HealthDataStore; views receive the store via environment.

Output: GooseAppModel.healthStore is a non-optional constant; GooseSwiftApp injects it; AppShellView is a passive consumer.
</objective>
