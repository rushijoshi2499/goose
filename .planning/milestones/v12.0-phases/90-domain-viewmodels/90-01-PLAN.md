across GooseAppModel. These are pure data containers — no logic, no BLE calls, no upload calls.
They establish the contracts that Plan 02 (GooseAppModel migration) and Plan 04 (view migration)
depend on.

that observes the changed domain object. A 1 Hz HR update (BLEState) must not invalidate views
that only read SyncState or HealthState.
