All communication occurs exclusively over Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) between the user's iOS device and the user's own WHOOP hardware.

## Legal Basis — European Union

Users in the European Union and Portugal benefit from the following legal protections, which take precedence over any contractual terms of service:

**Right to Data Portability (GDPR Art. 20)**
Under Regulation (EU) 2016/679, individuals have the right to receive their personal data — including biometric data — in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format. Contractual clauses that prevent a data subject from accessing data generated by their own body and collected on their own hardware are unenforceable under EU law.

**Interoperability Exception (Directive 2009/24/EC, Art. 6)**
EU software law permits the analysis of program interfaces without authorisation from the rights holder when the purpose is to achieve interoperability with an independently created program, the necessary information is not otherwise available, and the analysis is limited to the parts required for that interoperability. Analysis of BLE communication protocols for the purpose of personal data portability falls within this exception.
